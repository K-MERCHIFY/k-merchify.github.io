ITZY Fanmeet

MANILA, Philippines – Filipino MIDZYs, ready to see ITZY up close again? The ticket prices and seat plan for the group’s fan meeting in Manila have been announced on Wednesday, March 15. Tickets range from P1,000 for the general admission section to P8,500 for the VIP section. According to shop.bench.com, fans will get a ticket after having a “single-receipt net purchase of any regular-priced Bench & Herbench merchandise, cosmetics, selected consignment items, regular and promo bundles, and gift boxes and gift packs.”
